Homixidegang

Homixide Gang is an American rap duo consisting of members Keyon Thomas (born: January 4, 2000 [age 24]) and Demetrius Chatman (born: October 10, 2000 [age 23]).

Thomas goes by the stage name Homixide Beno!, while Chatman goes by Homixide Meechie. The members are from Atlanta, Georgia, as they are affiliated with rapper Playboi Carti's label, Opium.
